About Us

Yi Run Co., LT is a leading provider of study abroad services for international students. With years of experience, we specialize in assisting students who wish to pursue higher education in Canada. Our dedicated team provides comprehensive guidance on program selection, application processes, and scholarship opportunities, ensuring a smooth transition for students seeking to study abroad.

We work with top universities and colleges across Canada, helping students find the best academic fit based on their goals and interests. Our mission is to make the study abroad experience as seamless as possible, providing ongoing support before, during, and after arrival in Canada.

Whether you're looking to study in one of Canada's world-renowned universities or seeking a specialized program, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
